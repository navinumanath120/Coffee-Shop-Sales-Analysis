Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to understand sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product trends.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue patterns, peak sales hours, and top-performing products to support better business decisions.

---

## Dataset
Dataset used: Coffee Shop Sales Data

The dataset contains:

- Transaction details  
- Product categories (coffee types)  
- Order timestamps (hour, day, month)  
- Sales amount and quantity  
- Payment type (Card/Cash)  

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Data Visualization  

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Sales overview analysis  
- Time-based analysis (hourly, daily, monthly trends)  
- Product performance analysis  
- Payment method analysis  
- Trend identification across different time periods  

---

## Visualizations

- Revenue Share by Product

---

## Key Insights
- Total Sales reached **112.25K** with **3,547 orders**  
- Sales peak during **mid-morning hours (~10 AM)**  
- Afternoon and night contribute significantly to revenue  
- **Latte and Americano** are the top-selling products  
- Card payments are more commonly used than cash  
- Sales vary across weekdays and months
